direction, and stray dots floated around the sprite. Measured causes and
the fix are documented in
[`../../../playtests/2026-07-21-mage-sprite-fix/README.md`](../../../playtests/2026-07-21-mage-sprite-fix/README.md)

and per-clip `fit: "fixed"` scales sharing a 46.71px body height across all
