## Observation notes

For each notable hesitation or reaction, record:

- step and approximate elapsed time;
- what the player tried before asking for help;
- exact help required, if any;
- repeated backtracking or accidental input;
- unreadable text or unclear feedback;
- delight, boredom, frustration, or voluntary continued exploration;
- whether optional bonuses felt optional;
- initial classification: blocker or polish.

Do not redesign during the session. Capture what happened first.

## Acceptance gate

The current slice is ready to continue without a blocker fix when both profiles:

- complete the Waking Gate through normal input;
- discover movement and ACTION without repeated coaching;
- identify the objective and reach Mira;
- complete Mira's first errand without progression help;
- can answer or leave every optional prompt without blocking adventure;
- understand the Practice Slime's three-hit interaction;
- retain quest progress and rewards after reload;
- have no physical-iPad load, touch, safe-area, or readability blocker.

Fix demonstrated blockers, unreadable controls, save loss, and repeated confusion before treating the device experience as certified. Send curriculum, story, economy, reward-balance, or broader kid-UX findings to ChatGPT for product review.
